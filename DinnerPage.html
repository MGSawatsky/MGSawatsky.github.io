Chicken Fajitas



Ingredients

  • 2-3 Medium Chicken Breasts
  • 1/2 Bell Pepper
  • 1/2 Onion

Steps

  • Cut up the Chicken into cubes. Put Chicken cubes into a bowl with a bit of water and some seasoning as per your choice. Let sit for a few minutes
  • Dice or slice up the bell pepper and the onion.
  • Use a frying pan and turn heat to medium. Once heated up add chicken with water mixture.
  • Once water has been boiled off add the pepper and onion.
  • Cook until Chicken is done and veggies are cooked to satisfaction
  • Serve with Salsa, Lettuce, Shredded Cheese, Beans, and whatever else you want
  • Makes 2-3 Servings
