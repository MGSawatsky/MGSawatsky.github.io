Chicken Fajitas



Ingredients

  • 2-3 Medium Chicken Breasts
  • 1/2 Bell Pepper
  • 1/2 Onion

Steps

  • Cut up the Chicken into cubes. Put Chicken cubes into a bowl with a bit of water and some seasoning as per your choice. Let sit for a few minutes
  • Dice or slice up the bell pepper and the onion.
  • Use a frying pan and turn heat to medium. Once heated up add chicken with water mixture.
  • Once water has been boiled off add the pepper and onion.
  • Cook until Chicken is done and veggies are cooked to satisfaction
  • Serve with Salsa, Lettuce, Shredded Cheese, Beans, and whatever else you want
  • Makes 2-3 Servings

Fettuccine Alfredo


Ingredients

  • Fettuccine Noodles
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • 2 Tbsp Flour
  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/8 tsp Pepper
  • 2/3 Cup Grated Paramesan Cheese

Steps

  • Cook noodles in boiling water until done
  • For the sauce melt butter in a saucepan on medium heat
  • Add in flour. Heat and stir for 1 minute
  • Slowly add the next 3 ingredients stirring constantly until smooth
  • Heat and stir for 5-10 minutes until boiling and thickened
  • Remove from heat and add cheese stir until melted.
  • Makes ~2 cups

Tacos


Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of ground beef
  • 1/2 Bell Pepper
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 tsp of taco seasoning

Steps

  • Dice or slice up the bell pepper and the onion.
  • Mix water and taco seasoning together along with any other spices you desire
  • Brown the ground beef on a frying pan. Add onion and pepper.
  • Once browned add water mixture to pan. Cooked until water has been boiled off
  • Once water has been boiled off cook until veggies are done to satisfaction.
  • Serve with Salsa, Lettuce, Shredded Cheese, Beans, and whatever else you want
  • Makes 2-3 Servings
