Grilled Cheese


Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Sliced cheese

Steps

  • Heat a pan on the stove on medium heat
  • Spread some butter on the outside pieces of bread
  • Put Cheese inbetween the bread and put on the pan
  • Grilled both sides of the sandwich evenly until golden brown

Mac & Cheese


Ingredients

  • 2 cups of pasta
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese
  • 3 tbsp of butter
  • 1/4 cup of flour
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 dry mustard
  • 1/4 tsp of pepper
  • dash of paprika
  • 2 1/2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of cooked peas

Steps

  • Boil and drain pasta
  • Over medium heat melt butter
  • Add in flour through paprika, stir for about 3 minutes.
  • Slowly mix in milk
  • Remove from heat and add about 3/4's of the cheese. Stir until melted
  • Add to pasta and peas in an 8x8 dish
  • Sprinkle the rest of cheese on top
  • Bake at 375 F for 20-25 minutes

Zwieback


Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Milk
  • 1 cup of Butter
  • 2 1/4 tsps of dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 6 cups of flour

Steps

  • Dissolve yeast in water and sugar and let rise
  • Heat up milk and in a large bowl combine milk, butter, yeast and salt
  • Beat in enough flour to make a stiff batter, add more flour to make a smooth soft dough. Knead well
  • Cover and let rise until dough doubles in bulk
  • Form buns into a small ball. Place a smaller bun on top of a larger one, and press down firmly with one finger so that the two stick together
  • Place onto a greased pan and let rise until doubled
  • Bake in 350 F oven for about 15-20 minutes
