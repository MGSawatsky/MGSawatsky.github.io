Cookies


Ingredients

  • 1 cup of margarine (melted)
  • 2 cups of brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp of baking soda
  • 2 cups of rolled oats
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup of shredded coconut

Steps

  • Cream butter and sugar together
  • Beat in 1 egg at a time and Add vanilla
  • Add remaining ingredients. Mix well
  • Put on greased baking sheets in small spheres
  • Bake in oven at 350 F for 8-10 minutes
  • Makes ~5 dozen

Brownie Pudding


Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 2 tbsp of cocoa
  • 2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 tbsp butter or margarine (melted)
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cocoa
  • 1 3/4 cup of boiling water

Steps

  • Mix first 5 ingredients together
  • Add milk, butter, and vanilla. Mix well
  • Move into a baking dish. Smooth top
  • Combine brown sugar and cocoa. Mix well and sprinkle over the top of the batter.
  • Pour water over top of everything
  • Bake at 350 F for 40 minutes or in the microwave for 10 minutes.

Lemon Squares


Ingredients

    Base

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 cup of icing sugar
  • 3/4 cup of butter

    • Filling

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1/3 cup of lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps

    Base

  • Combine flour and icing sugar. Blend well
  • Cut in butter until crumbly
  • press into greased 9x13 pan
  • Bake at 325 F for 15-20 minutes, until lightly brown

    • Filling

  • Beat eggs, sugar, lemon juice, flour, and baking powder until smooth
  • Pour over partially baked crust
  • Bake at 325 F for 25-30 or until set and golden
  • Sprinkle icing sugar over squares after they have cooled
  • Makes ~4 dozen
