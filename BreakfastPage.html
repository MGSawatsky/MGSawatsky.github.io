Waffles


Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups of flour
  • 4 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of margarine

Steps

  • Mix dry ingredients in a bowl together.
  • In a separate bowl mix the wet ingredients. Make sure to melt the margarine before mixing in.
  • Pour the wet mixture slowly into the dry mixture. Mix well
  • Use batter in a waffle iron.
  • Makes ~6 whole waffles

Crepes


Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  • Mix all wet ingredients and salt.
  • Add flour and beat until smooth
  • Pour 1/2 cup of batter on pan over medium heat
  • Tilt pan to coat surface evenly
  • Cook for 2 minutes until light brown and turn and cook other side.
  • Makes ~4 crepes
